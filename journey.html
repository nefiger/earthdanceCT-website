The Four Chapters

One story, four gatherings

01HEART

Heart

Love in a Bowl, Hout Bay · complete

Heart opened the journey through warmth, community, conversation and place.

The first gathering took place at Love in a Bowl in Hout Bay: a living community space rooted in food, care, learning and local connection. It was a family-friendly day that brought people together gently and reminded us that Earthdance is not only about the final festival weekend. It is also about the relationships that make that weekend meaningful.

Heart gave the year its emotional grounding. It was a real meeting point between communities — and it set the tone for everything that follows: Earthdance grows through relationship, not only promotion.

Read the Heart story Follow Love in a Bowl
02BEAT

Beat

Saturday 18 July 2026 · Leopard's Rest Hall, Hout Bay

If Heart grounded the journey, Beat raises the pulse.

Beat is the night chapter: rhythm, movement, dancefloor energy and Sonic Horizon spirit. It carries the music more directly and brings a stronger sense of momentum as we move deeper into the season — a dancefloor-focused gathering rooted in trance, psytrance, rhythm, movement and connection.

Beat is not separate from Earthdance. It is one of the ways the journey gathers force on the way to September.

  • When Sat 18 July 2026, 20:00–02:00
  • Where Leopard's Rest Hall, 31 Harbour Rd, Hout Bay
  • Tickets from R50 on Quicket
  • Age no under-18s

Humanity

Humanity gathers the threads.

After Heart and Beat, this chapter brings the journey back into a wider sense of togetherness, expression and shared humanity. It is the point where the emotional, musical and communal parts of the path begin to converge before the main September gathering.

04EARTHDANCE

Earthdance

18–20 September 2026 · Kromrivier Farm, Western Cape

From 18–20 September 2026, the journey arrives at Kromrivier Farm.

This is the main gathering: a multi-day camping festival shaped by music, participation, gathering spaces, market life and the shared rhythm of community in spring.

At its centre is the Prayer for Peace at 01:00 SAST on Saturday 19 September, when Earthdance communities around the world link up in a synchronised global moment.

That is the heartbeat at the centre of everything.

  • When 18–20 September 2026, gates 12:00 Friday
  • Where Kromrivier Farm, between Greyton and Riviersonderend
  • Linkup Prayer for Peace, 01:00 SAST on Saturday 19 September
  • Age no under-18s

Why the Journey Matters

Earthdance begins before September

The warm-up gatherings help us build community, trust, momentum, story and participation.

Each one exists for its own reason. Heart put relationships before promotion. Beat puts the music and the dancefloor back at the centre. Humanity draws the circle wider before we head to the farm.

They make the final convergence more alive, more grounded and more real. If you arrive at Kromrivier in September having walked some of this path, you will not be arriving at a festival. You will be arriving at a meeting point.
