Glamping & camping

Earthdance is a multi-day camping gathering, and spending the weekend on site is part of the experience. Whether you bring your own setup or walk into a ready-made tent with the bed already dressed, this is where you choose your home for the weekend.

Staying at Earthdance

Wake up inside the gathering

Camping keeps people close to the rhythm of the weekend: the music, the mornings, the slower moments in between, and the shared atmosphere of being together in one place. You don't drive home from Earthdance at 2am — you walk back to your tent under the stars, and the farm is still there when you unzip in the morning.

For 2026 the stay range runs from bring-your-own camping with your car beside your tent, through ready-set canvas glamping tents for two, to a private cottage. Every option is described below.

The Options in Detail

Choose your home for the weekend

01BYO

BYO Camp + Car Camping

Bring your own tent and camp next to your car

For guests who prefer their own camping setup but want the convenience of keeping their vehicle close by.

It is a practical, flexible choice for those arriving with their own tent, mattress, bedding, chairs and camping equipment. This option does not include a supplied tent, mattress, bedding or shade structure — you are responsible for your full camping setup.

  • Includes managed camping area, shared social space, charging points, staff support
  • Best for self-sufficient campers who want convenience, vehicle access and the lowest-cost option
022.5M

Canvas Glamping 2.5m

A ready-set canvas tent for two, with bedding included

A simple, comfortable festival stay without needing to bring your own tent or bedding.

Your waterproof canvas tent is set up before you arrive and includes mattresses, fitted sheets, comforters and pillow cases. This is the most compact glamping option — suited to guests who want a pre-built sleeping space while keeping things practical and affordable.

  • Includes waterproof 2.5m canvas tent, mattresses, bedding, rechargeable tent light, charging points, shared social area, staff support
  • Best for two guests who want an easy, ready-made setup without unnecessary extras
033M

Canvas Glamping 3m

A roomier ready-set canvas tent for a more comfortable weekend

A larger waterproof canvas tent with mattresses and bedding already provided.

A good fit for two people who want a little more space inside the tent for bags, movement and a more comfortable festival base. Your tent is set up before arrival and includes fitted sheets, comforters and pillow cases.

  • Includes waterproof 3m canvas tent, mattresses, bedding, rechargeable tent light, charging points, shared social area, staff support
  • Best for two guests who want a more spacious glamping tent and a lower-effort stay
042.5M+
Shade pending supplier confirmation

Shaded Canvas Glamping 2.5m

A compact canvas glamping tent with added shade

The same ready-set 2.5m waterproof canvas tent, mattresses and bedding — with the added benefit of shade over the tent.

Designed for guests who want the ease of glamping plus a cooler, more protected tent environment during the day. Bedding includes fitted sheets, comforters and pillow cases.

  • Includes waterproof 2.5m canvas tent, mattresses, bedding, shade over the tent, rechargeable tent light, charging points, shared social area, staff support
  • Best for two guests who want a compact glamping setup with extra daytime comfort
053M+
Shade pending supplier confirmation

Shaded Canvas Glamping 3m

A larger canvas glamping tent with added shade

The most comfortable tented festival stay in the accommodation range.

A larger waterproof canvas tent with mattresses, bedding and shade over the tent, giving guests more space and better daytime protection. Bedding includes fitted sheets, comforters and pillow cases.

  • Includes waterproof 3m canvas tent, mattresses, bedding, shade over the tent, rechargeable tent light, charging points, shared social area, staff support
  • Best for two guests who want the roomiest tented option with added shade and comfort
06STAY

Morado Cottage for Two

A private cottage stay for two guests

The most private and comfortable accommodation option — a proper indoor stay while remaining close to the Earthdance weekend experience.

Ideal for guests who prefer not to camp, or who want a quieter base with more privacy than the tented accommodation options.

  • Includes private cottage accommodation for two guests
  • Best for a couple, close friends, or guests wanting the highest-comfort option
Please note: the shared social area includes tables and access to charging points, but no kitchen equipment is provided. Guests must bring their own food-preparation items, including plates, cutlery, knives, cups, cookware and any other equipment they may need.
