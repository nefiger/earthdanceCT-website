Glamping & camping

Earthdance is a multi-day camping gathering, and spending the weekend on site is part of the experience. Whether you bring your own setup or walk into a ready-made tent with the bed already dressed, this is where you choose your home for the weekend.

Staying at Earthdance

Wake up inside the gathering

Camping keeps people close to the rhythm of the weekend: the music, the mornings, the slower moments in between, and the shared atmosphere of being together in one place. You don't drive home from Earthdance at 2am — you walk back to your tent under the stars, and the farm is still there when you unzip in the morning.

For 2026 the accommodation range is confirmed and bookable now on Quicket, on the same event page as festival tickets: bring-your-own camping with your car beside your tent from R350, and ready-set canvas glamping tents for two from R3,000. Every option is described below.

BYO Camp & Park

Bring your own tent and camping setup, with space to camp next to your car

For guests who prefer their own camping setup but want the convenience of keeping their vehicle close by.

A practical, flexible choice for those arriving with their own tent, mattress, bedding, chairs and camping equipment. This option does not include a supplied tent, mattress, bedding or shade structure — you are responsible for your full camping setup.

  • Includes managed accommodation area, shared social space, charging points, staff support
  • Best for self-sufficient campers who want convenience, vehicle access and the lowest-cost option

A simple, comfortable festival stay without needing to bring your own tent or bedding.

Your ready-set waterproof canvas tent is up before you arrive, with mattresses, fitted sheet, comforter and pillow cases included. This is the most compact glamping option — a pre-built sleeping space that keeps things practical and affordable.

  • Includes waterproof canvas tent for two, mattresses, bedding, rechargeable tent light, charging access, shared social space, staff support

How Booking Works

Clear options, no guesswork

All accommodation is booked through Quicket, on the same event page as festival tickets — choose your stay option when you book.

  • Each option is listed separately with its own price and inclusions — check the Quicket listing for full details.
  • Glamping tents sleep two guests each, and capacity is limited: if you want one, book early.
  • Accommodation guests will receive arrival and check-in guidance closer to the event, and the same information will be published here.
Before You Book

Know what you're walking into

Glamping covers your bed, not your kitchen: the shared social space has tables and charging points, but you bring your own plates, cutlery, cups and cookware.

The BYO Camp & Park option covers the managed accommodation area with your car beside your tent — your full camping setup is yours to bring.

Whatever you choose, read Practical Info for what to pack: spring on the farm means warm days and properly cold nights.
