Volunteer with Earthdance

Earthdance is built by people. If you want to help shape the gathering from the inside, we would love to hear from you.

Photo: Alison Swan Photography
Why Volunteer

Build it, don't just attend it

Volunteering at Earthdance is a way to participate in building the event, not just attending it.

Some people come to support the practical work. Some want to contribute their energy, skill or care. Some want to be part of the crew that helps hold the space together.

All of that matters.

Where Help May Be Needed

Volunteer areas

Build & setup

Getting the site ready in the days before gates open.

Decor & site dressing

Making the farm feel like Earthdance.

Gate & arrivals

Welcoming people well and getting them in smoothly.

Guest & crew support

Holding the human side of the gathering across the weekend.

Cleanup & pack-down

Leaving the farm the way we found it — or better.

General operational support

The unglamorous work that makes everything else possible.

Specific role needs may change as the event gets closer.

Who We Are Looking For

Attitude counts as much as experience

We are looking for people who are:

  • reliable
  • communicative
  • practical
  • respectful
  • willing to work as part of a team
  • aligned with the spirit of the event

Previous festival experience can help, but it is not the only thing that matters. Good attitude, commitment and follow-through matter just as much.

What To Expect

A real commitment

Volunteering is a real commitment. Depending on the role, you may be asked to support:

  • before the event
  • during the event
  • after the event

Workload, timing and exchange details will depend on the role and the phase of planning.

Still being confirmed: volunteer exchange, shift structure and timing details are TBC and will be shared with applicants as roles are matched.
The Process

From interest to crew

What to include in your application

  • your name
  • contact details
  • where you are based
  • relevant skills or experience
  • what kind of role you are interested in
  • your availability around the event
  1. ApplySend through your details, availability, skills and any role preferences via the volunteer form.
  2. ReviewWe review interest based on current needs and crew fit.
  3. MatchIf there is a match, we will get in touch with next steps, expectations and timing.
